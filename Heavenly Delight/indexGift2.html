About Heaven's Delight

About the Founder

I’m Gift Kamashy, the founder of Heaven’s Delight. I’m a certified birth doula, a devoted Christian, and a woman with a heart deeply called to serve expecting mothers through the sacred journey of childbirth.

I founded Heaven’s Delight on the unshakable foundation of Christ. My mission is to make sure every woman I serve feels seen, heard, safe, and spiritually uplifted. Through birth doula services, prayer sessions, emotional and support, I walk with you — offering not just knowledge and comfort, but faith-filled presence every step of the way.

Our Service

Our Mission

At Heaven’s Delight, we offer more than birth support — we offer presence, peace, and personal touches that honor this sacred journey. Whether preparing for your first child or growing your family, our services are rooted in compassion, prayer, and faith.

Birth Doula Services

  • Two prenatal visits to prepare your mind, body, and spirit
  • 24/7 on-call support beginning at 38 weeks
  • Continuous labor support at your home and/or birth location
  • Physical comfort techniques (breathing, massage, positioning)
  • Optional spiritual encouragement (prayer, Scripture, worship music)
  • Package fee: $1700

Prayer Support

Faith is the foundation of everything we offer. Prayer and spiritual support are gently woven into your care, as desired.

Personalized Care

Every family’s journey is unique. We aim to honor your story with presence, compassion, and thoughtful attention, offering services shaped by both expertise and faith.
